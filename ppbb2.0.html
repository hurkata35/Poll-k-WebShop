story of the creation:
In the spring of 1935, my parents and I went to live with my uncle, my mother's brother, in his little town on the Moselle, where my father was going to find work. My uncle's name was Louis Hacquard, and he had a very large family. He had a wife who was a widow and five children by a previous marriage. With my mother, this brought the number of souls under his roof to twelve. My father died a few months later, and I am still grateful that Uncle Louis, whose own affairs were hardly prosperous, never turned me away.
